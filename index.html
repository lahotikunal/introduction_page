Lucky 7

By Kunal:-)

Welcome to Lucky 7.!

  • The value of the bid is fixed 15 Lakhs, and the initial sum of 90 Lakhs is given , select one of <7 , =7 or >7 and, roll the dice.
  • Outcomes of different choices
    • Winning twice the amount :- If you select one of <7 and the sum of dice less than 7, you select one of >7 and the sum of dice more than 7.
    • Winning thrice the amount :- if you select =7 and sum of dice is equal to 7.
    • Reduction is net worth eual to bid value :- If you select one of <7 and the sum of dice more than 7, you select one of >7 and the sum of dice less than 7.
  • You will be given a option of insurance at a certain point, if you choose to select insurance your loss will be compensated(no money will be removed in case of loss) but an insurance premium will be removed from your total amount at each roll , but if you win you get the winning amount minus the premium amount added to the total sum.
    • Have Fun!
