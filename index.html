Lucky 7

By Kunal:-)

Welcome to Lucky 7.!

  • The bid value is set at 15 lakhs, while the initial sum is set at 90 lakhs. Choose one of the numbers 7, =7, or >7, and roll the dice.
  • Outcomes of different choices
    • Winning twice the amount :- a) If you choose '<7' and the total number of dice is less than 7, or b) If you choose'>7' and the total number of dice is greater than 7.
    • Winning thrice the amount :- If you choose =7, the total of the dice is 7.
    • Reduction is net worth equal to bid value :- a) If you choose '<7' and the total number of dice is greater than 7, or b) if you choose'>7' and the total number of dice is less than 7.
  • If insurance is chosen, no money will be removed in the event of a loss, but the premium will be deducted. If you win, however, you will receive the winnings minus the premium.
